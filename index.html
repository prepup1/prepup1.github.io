About Us

At Prep Up, we enable our students to enhance, enrich and develop their skills through unconventional means. We believe that the best way to learn and retain information is through hands-on activities; which is why we have incorporated demonstrations and simple experiments as part of our lessons. This not only makes the lessons more enjoyable for the students, but also helps with better information retention. Our methods help students prepare for examinations better. Request a call back or visit our centre for more information. Happy Learning!
